# HackOnVibe — Project Questionnaire

**1. What does your application/service do?**

Heckle is an AI-powered mobile app launch simulator. It rehearses your launch by simulating realistic public reactions to your app's launch copy before you publish it. A gauntlet of up to 6 independent AI personas — Early Adopter, Mobile Tech Journalist, Social Media Scroller, App Investor, App Store Browser, and First-Time User — each critique your asset through a different lens. A second AI pass then produces a Launch Impact Scorecard (download intent, shareability, media potential, store conversion), a consensus analysis, concrete rewrite suggestions, and a promotion coverage map.

**2. Who is the target audience?**

Indie hackers, early-stage founders, product managers writing internal pitches, and students entering startup competitions — anyone crafting launch copy (App Store descriptions, Product Hunt posts, TikTok/Reels captions, tweet threads, press releases, cold emails to journalists, or investor pitches) who wants private, structured feedback before going public.

**5. What is your advantage?**

Heckle lets founders pressure-test and fix their launch messaging privately — before thousands of people see it — instead of discovering fatal flaws after launch. Each of the 6 personas reviews independently, with no shared prompt and no canned responses, through a distinct real-consumer lens, and the tool returns structured, actionable output: an impact scorecard, cross-critic consensus, concrete rewrites, and a promotion map. Its editorial, magazine-inspired design deliberately avoids generic "AI app" aesthetics.
